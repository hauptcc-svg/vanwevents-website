- Dynamic animations

## 🚀 Quick Start (Next 24 Hours)

### Step 1: Deploy to Vercel (30 minutes)
1. Create Vercel account at https://vercel.com
2. Upload or connect your GitHub repo with `landing-page.html`
3. Vercel automatically detects and deploys
4. Domain: vanwevents.co.za is set up in Vercel settings

### Step 2: Customize Placeholders (1 hour)
- [ ] Replace social media links with your Instagram, Facebook, TikTok URLs
- [ ] Update pricing once approved by payment gateway
- [ ] Replace placeholder images with MSC cruise photos (from press area)
- [ ] Add your promotional video (WhatsApp videos in folder)

### Step 3: Test Everything (30 minutes)
- [ ] Test on desktop, tablet, mobile
- [ ] Click all links and scroll all sections
- [ ] Try form submissions (success messages will appear)
- [ ] Check email display

### Step 4: Go Live 🎉
Your landing page is live at vanwevents.co.za!

## 🆘 Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit the landing page after deployment?**
A: Yes! Update landing-page.html and redeploy to Vercel.

**Q: How do I add my promotional video?**
A: Add it to your Vercel project assets and reference in the HTML.

**Q: Will the forms work without Resend?**
A: Yes, they'll show success messages but won't send emails until Resend is integrated.

**Q: Can I change the colors?**
A: Yes! Search for #FF6B35 (orange) in the HTML and replace with your brand color.

**Q: How do I track which emails opted in?**
A: Resend dashboard automatically logs all email activity.
